About Us

NGO Help is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating a better future for underprivileged communities across India. Founded in 2020, we believe that every person deserves access to basic needs like food, education, and healthcare.

With the support of volunteers, donors, and partners, we have been able to reach thousands of families and bring positive change to society. Our team works with full transparency and commitment to serve humanity.

Our Mission & Vision

🎯 Our Mission

To uplift marginalized communities by providing essential resources and opportunities for a dignified life.

🌍 Our Vision

A world where no child sleeps hungry, no student lacks education, and no patient is denied healthcare.

💡 Our Values

Compassion, Integrity, Transparency, and Service to Humanity.

Our Programs

We run multiple programs to support people in need:

🍎 Hunger Free India

Providing daily meals and monthly ration kits to 1000+ families every month.

📚 Education For All

Free tuition centers, school supplies, and scholarships for underprivileged children.

🏥 Health & Wellness

Free medical camps, health checkups, and medicines in rural villages.

🌳 Environment

Tree plantation drives and cleanliness campaigns to build a greener future.
